A Note on Mobile E-Commerce

If you don’t have a store that is accessible through a phone or that is optimized for a mobile device, you will have a much harder time getting people to purchase items from your store especially if it is an item that they can find from another retailer. Mobile-optimized online stores feature quick and easy checkouts, one tap navigator, bigger images and intuitive interfaces that respond to touch scrolling. By creating an online store that can be accessed from a tablet or smartphone, you likely reach thousands of new customers each month.
